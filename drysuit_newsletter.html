Why Drysuit Diving?
Drysuit diving extends seasonal boundaries, providing a year-round opportunity to explore the hidden wonders of the Mediterranean underwaters. Here's why drysuit diving is your key to unlocking the extraordinary:

  1. Extended Dive Seasons: With a drysuit, you're not limited to warmer months. Experience the magic of the Mediterranean even when the temperatures drop. Our course equips you with the skills to enjoy the underwater world in all its seasonal splendor.
  2. Longer Dives: Drysuit diving allows for extended underwater excursions in comfort, giving you more time to marvel at the diverse marine life and stunning landscapes that Portofino has to offer.
  3. Exploration: Dive deeper with confidence. The buoyancy redundancy provided by drysuits and the termal insulation helps you to explore the depths of the Marine Protected Area, revealing hidden treasures.
  4. Enhanced Safety: Safety is paramount. Our top-of-the-range equipment and expert instructors ensure that you dive with the utmost confidence, combining the thrill of exploration with a commitment to your well-being.

Embark on a year-round underwater adventure with our exclusive Drysuit Course, set against the stunning backdrop of Portofino's Marine Protected Area. Immerse yourself in the beauty of the Mediterranean, where drysuit diving isn't just for winter, it's a gateway to longer, deeper dives with enhanced safety.

What Awaits You

  • Underwater Wonderland: Explore the Mediterranean as you dive through the Marine Protected Area of Portofino.
  • Top-of-the-Range Equipment: Elevate your diving experience with our premium BARE drysuits. Our latest gear ensures comfort and allows you to extend your dive time and explore with confidence.
  • Expert Guidance: Learn from our experienced instructors who are not only passionate about diving but also familiar with the unique underwater landscape of Portofino and drysuit diving.

Who we address

  • Qualified Advanced Open Water divers or equivalent level of diving certification
  • Active divers who have dived in recent times
  • Capable divers with solid basic skills

Want to participate but you don’t fit in the prerequisites? We can schedule a PADI Advanced OWD course or a Scuba Refresher prior to the conservation diving workshop.
